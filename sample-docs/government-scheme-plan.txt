LOCATION DETAILS:
-----------------
Village: Rampur
District: Pune
State: Maharashtra
Block: Haveli
Population Covered: 2,500 residents
Total Households: 450

PROJECT OVERVIEW:
-----------------
This comprehensive sanitation project aims to achieve 100% sanitation coverage 
in Rampur village through construction of individual household latrines (IHHL), 
community sanitary complexes, and solid waste management infrastructure. The 
project aligns with Swachh Bharat Mission objectives and will ensure proper 
waste disposal, water supply to toilets, and community awareness programs.

PHASE-WISE IMPLEMENTATION PLAN:
================================

PHASE 1: FOUNDATION AND INITIAL CONSTRUCTION (Months 1-3)
----------------------------------------------------------
Timeline: 01/01/2025 to 31/03/2025
Budget Allocated: Rs. 20,00,000

Planned Activities:
1. Site Survey and Beneficiary Identification
   - Complete household survey of 450 families
   - Identify 200 beneficiaries for Phase 1
   - Geo-tagging of construction sites
   - Soil testing for foundation work

2. Material Procurement
   - Cement: 500 bags
   - Sand: 100 cubic meters
   - Bricks: 50,000 units
   - Steel rods: 2 tons
   - Pipes and fittings: As per technical specifications

3. Construction Work
   - Complete construction of 100 Individual Household Latrines (IHHL)
   - Foundation work for 100 more units
   - Installation of twin-pit pour-flush latrines
   - Water connection to 100 units

4. Quality Assurance
   - Inspection by Block Engineer - Weekly
   - Quality testing of materials
   - Photographic documentation
   - GPS coordinates recording

Milestones:
- Week 2: Survey completion and beneficiary list finalization
- Week 4: Material procurement completed
- Week 8: 50 IHHL construction completed
- Week 12: 100 IHHL fully completed and functional

Deliverables:
- 100 functional household toilets
- Foundation ready for next 100 units
- Detailed progress report with photos
- Utilization certificate for Phase 1 budget

   - Wall painting with sanitation messages

Milestones:

- Week 20: Community toilet complex foundation complete
- Week 24: Community complex fully functional

Deliverables:
- 200 household toilets completed (cumulative total)
- 1 functional community sanitary complex
- 2 compost pits operational
- 50 waste collection bins distributed
- IEC materials distributed to all households

QUALITY STANDARDS:
==================
1. All construction as per Central Public Health and Environmental Engineering Organisation (CPHEEO) guidelines
2. Twin-pit pour-flush toilet design mandatory
3. Minimum pit size: 1.5m x 1.0m x 1.3m
4. Cement-sand mortar ratio: 1:6
5. Minimum 7 days curing period
6. Water availability within 50 meters of each toilet
7. Proper ventilation in all structures

MONITORING & EVALUATION:
========================
1. Weekly site visits by Block Development Officer
2. Monthly review meetings at District level
3. Quarterly evaluation by State-level team
4. GPS-based monitoring system
5. Photographic documentation at each milestone

7. Grievance redressal mechanism through toll-free number

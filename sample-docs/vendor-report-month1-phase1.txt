CHALLENGES FACED:
=================
1. Water Scarcity (MINOR)
   - Issue: Shortage of water for construction during dry spell
   - Impact: 2 days delay in concrete curing
   - Resolution: Arranged water tanker supply from nearby source
   - Status: RESOLVED

2. Local Festival (MINOR)
   - Issue: Republic Day and local festival reduced working days
   - Impact: 3 working days lost
   - Resolution: Increased workforce and worked extra hours
   - Status: COMPENSATED

3. Material Transport Delay (MINOR)
   - Issue: Cement delivery delayed by 3 days due to supplier stock issue
   - Impact: Minor slowdown in construction
   - Resolution: Used advance stock, no work stoppage
   - Status: RESOLVED

No major challenges encountered. Project running smoothly.

CORRECTIVE ACTIONS TAKEN:
